Data Analysis {# My Portfolio#} About project Swagger

    What's this project about

  • The main purpose of this application is to allow investors to overview how the currency risk affects their investments.

  • Currency risk arises from the change in price from one currency in relation to another.

  • TRY/USD change of 10% in span of 3 months

Tools available

  • Exchange rates chart - for more than 100 currencies

  • Stock price chart - for all stock

  • Stock price chart - in any currency with historic exchange rates

  • Currency risk visualization

Target Audience

  • All investors who want to invest in foreign currency

  • Application is highly customizable and can be tailored for your needs

  • In future expansion outside of the stock market

Technologies used

  • Flask - http://flask.pocoo.org/

  • Chart.js - https://www.chartjs.org/

  • Swagger - https://app.swaggerhub.com/welcome

API used

  • Alpha Vantage - https://www.alphavantage.co/

  • Open Exchange Rate - https://openexchangerates.org/
